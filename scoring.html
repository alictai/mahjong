Final Notes

Score of the winning hand is calculated as 2^fan.

The player who went mahjong is paid by the other players the amount scored by their hand.

If a player threw out the winning tile, that player owes the winner double.

If a player threw out the winning tile while the winner had 3 same-suit sets revealed ("rushing"), and the winner has an all same suit (no honor tiles) hand, then the player has to pay for everyone else at the table.
