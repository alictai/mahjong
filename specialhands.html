In addition to the scoring system, there are also the following special hands. These are difficult to compose, but are each worth the maximum fan.

Nine Gates

Three 1's, a sequence of 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, and three 9's, all of the same suit, and any other tile of the same suit completing the hand. The hand must be concealed, but the winning tile can be a discard. No Kongs are allowed.

Thirteen Orphans

One of each Dragon and Wind, 1 and 9 of each suit, and 14th tile forming a pair with any of these. Hand must be concealed, but the winning tile can be a discard.

Heavenly Hands

Player who is first in turn order (East) mahjongs on dealt hand.

Earthly Hands

Player who is not first in turn order (South, West, or North) mahjongs on the first player's discard.
